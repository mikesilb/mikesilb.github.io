Mike's Favorite Hobbies

The rock band,RUSH

RUSH is a three-membered progressive rock band from Toronto, ON, Canada. They consist of singer and bassist, Geddy Lee, guitarist, Alex Lifeson, and drummer Neil Peart. The band has been in existence for over 40 years. Their music is technically as sharp as it comes and their sound takes you to a different galaxy altogether.

The Los Angeles Lakers

I have been a Laker fan since 1981 as I was raised in Los Angeles. I have seen three different championship dynasties in my lifetime:

  1. The Showtime Years (1980-1987). Notable players include Earvin Magic Johnson, Kareem Abdul Jabbar, James Worthy, and Michael Cooper. Championships include 1980, 1982, 1985, and 1987.
  2. The Shaq-Kobe Years (1996-2004). Notable players include Shaquille O'Neal, Kobe Bryant, Derek Fisher, Robert Horry, and Rick Fox. Championships include 2000, 2001, and 2002.
  3. The Kobe-Pau Years (2008-2011). Notable players include Kobe Bryant, Pau Gasol, Derek Fisher, Lamar Odom, and Andrew Bynum. Championships include 2009 and 2010.
